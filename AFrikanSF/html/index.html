The Afrikan STEM Force

We intend to help transform the future of the African continent by inculcating certain skills into not only the next generation, but interested individuals, groups and institutions as well.


Where building competence in using technology and engineering tools relevant to 21st-century challenges is concerned, we introduce students to careers in STEM fields. Not to mention supporting hands-on, experiential learning and the enhancement of technological literacy as executed by our facilitators who ensure the nurturing of creativity and innovation.
